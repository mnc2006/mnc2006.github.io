About Me

I needed a change. I was growing tired of the field I had thought I wanted to be apart of for the rest of my life. Biology. It was awesome then and still is to this day. But my interests were moving towards technology. And with that I had to realize that goals, career paths, and life changes as you grow older. So I started diving into the world of web development, mobile applications, cloud computing, web security... It all fascinated me. I took some courses where I learned my first programming language, Ruby. My goal is to help change the world through technology, one application at a time. I am excited about my career change and I know that it is an ever-growing field, so I can expand my knowledge base by learning multiple programming concepts.
