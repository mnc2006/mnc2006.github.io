About Me

Hello there and welcome to my website. I'm glad you decided to find out more about me. I decided to make a career change after spending much of my life in the sciences. My interests were always geared towards technology, but I thought of it as my hobby. I have never thought to take a much enjoyed past-time of mine and turn it into something I could do daily and feel fulfilled and rewarded. So I started diving into the world of web development, mobile applications, cloud computing, web security... It all fascinated me. I took some courses where I learned my first programming language, Ruby. I am still digging further and further into this new programming language because there is so much to learn and enjoy. My goal is to help change the world through technology, one application at a time. I am excited about my career change and I hope you are motivated to take that next step just as I did.
